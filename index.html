Summary: Latitude vs X

We seek to understand the relationship between Latitude and weather parameters.

Using citipy library, a random sample of cities is chosen. Then weather parameter data is obtained from OpenWeather API. Latitude is plotted against Max Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness, and Wind Speed. It appears that only Max Temperature has any correlation. To verify, plots are further broken into North and South hemispheres, and linear regression added. Max Temperature is only plot with significant R Squared, confirming correlation.
